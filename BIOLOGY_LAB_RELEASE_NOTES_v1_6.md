# Biology Concepts Lab — v1.6-stable Release Notes

## Focus

v1.6 adds a Board Answer Builder so Biology lessons convert naturally into structured exam-ready explanations, especially for Class 9 and Class 10.

## Main additions

- 1-mark, 2-mark, 3-mark, and 5-mark answer scaffolds.
- Strand-aware answer coaching for life basics, plants, animals, human body, health, nutrition, cell biology, microbes, ecosystems, environment, reproduction, genetics, evolution, diversity, and life processes.
- Diagram cue strips: what to label or describe for a board-style answer.
- Mechanism-chain prompts: input/process/output, structure/function/system, cause/effect/application.
- Model answer script that changes by class, strand, and mark target.
- Rubric cards for full credit, partial credit, and review-needed answers.
- Misconception correction embedded into higher-mark answers.

## Why this matters

The Biology Lab already had visual explanation and diagram practice. v1.6 closes the next gap: students often understand a concept visually but fail to write a clear exam answer. The new builder bridges observation, diagram, mechanism, example, and final answer.
