Biology Concepts Lab Class 1–10 Math + Physics + Chemistry aligned

Understand life as a living system.

A guided Biology learning lab for Class 1–10, upgraded to v1.3-stable. It keeps the same STEM Lab backbone as Math, Physics, and Chemistry, while giving Biology its own identity: living-system visuals, field notebook evidence, concept ladders, body/cell/ecosystem models, heredity, evolution, health, and conservation.

Student flow

Observation first, life-system model second, biology vocabulary third. The learner sees one concept at a time and moves from real-life observation to visual model, explanation, practice, and mini test.
