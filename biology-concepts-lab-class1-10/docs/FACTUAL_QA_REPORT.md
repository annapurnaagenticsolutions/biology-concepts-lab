# Biology Concepts Lab — Subject Evaluation and Factual QA

## Scope reviewed

- 40 lessons across Classes 1–10.
- Living/non-living evidence, plant systems, animal needs, clean habits, food chains, digestion, adaptations, nutrients, disease spread, cells, microbes, biodiversity, heredity, evolution, and life processes.

## Evaluation

- **Factuality:** Good. Most biological claims are school-appropriate and avoid over-claiming.
- **Age fit:** Strong. The lab starts with observation and moves toward systems, cells, heredity, and evolution.
- **Safety:** Good, especially around hygiene, food, plants, and field observation.
- **Main issue found:** Some plant-life statements were too general and needed caveats for non-flowering plants and non-seed reproduction.

## Applied fixes

1. Added flowering-plant caveats for flowers/fruits/seeds.
2. Improved root function wording: anchor plus absorb water/minerals.
3. Improved photosynthesis wording: green plant parts, light, carbon dioxide, water, sugar, oxygen, and respiration caveat.
4. Improved life-cycle wording to avoid over-generalising every plant lifecycle.
5. Added factual QA banner and docs.

## Verdict

Biology is deployable after this patch. It is conceptually strong and age-appropriate, with caveats now added where simplification risk was highest.
